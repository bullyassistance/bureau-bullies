Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026 · The Bureau Bullies, LLC · Wilmington, Delaware

This Privacy Policy explains what information The Bureau Bullies, LLC ("we," "us") collects, how we use it, and your rights. By using our services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: first name, last name, email, phone number.
  • Credit Information You Upload: any credit report, screenshot, or financial document you submit for analysis. This includes account numbers, balances, payment histories, inquiries, and personal identifiers contained in those documents.
  • Communications: messages exchanged with Bully AI, emails, SMS replies, and support tickets.
  • Payment Information: we do not store full credit card numbers. Payments are processed by Stripe (or similar PCI-compliant processor).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps, referring URLs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Perform the credit analysis you requested (Bully AI scan).
  • Send you your results, attack plan, and dispute letters.
  • Provide the products and services you purchase.
  • Send marketing and educational communications via SMS and email (with consent).
  • Improve our services and AI models in aggregate and de-identified form.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your credit information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers that help us run the business — including GoHighLevel (SMS + CRM), Stripe (payments), Anthropic (AI analysis), and our hosting provider. Each is contractually required to protect your data.
  • Our DFY team members working directly on your file, if you subscribe to Done-For-You service.
  • Law enforcement or courts if compelled by valid legal process.

4. AI Processing Notice

When you upload a credit report or any image, the content is transmitted to our AI analysis provider (currently Anthropic's Claude API) to generate your results. The AI provider's terms prohibit it from using your data to train its models without your consent. Analysis results are returned to us and associated with your contact record. We may retain a record of your analysis output for up to 24 months for quality, compliance, and re-analysis purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Raw uploaded files are deleted from our processing servers within 7 days of your scan.
  • Your Bully AI analysis output is retained for up to 24 months.
  • Your contact record and purchase history are retained as long as your account is active, and for up to 7 years thereafter for legal and accounting requirements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your state or country, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications (reply STOP to SMS or click unsubscribe in email).
  • Portability of your data to another provider.

To exercise any right, email support@bureaubullies.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. No system is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a breach involving your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Children

Our services are not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. SMS Program

By opting in to SMS, you consent to receive recurring promotional and transactional messages from The Bureau Bullies at the phone number you provide. Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel. Your carrier is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated via email and posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
